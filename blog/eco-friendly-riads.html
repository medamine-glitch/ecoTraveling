Top 5 Eco-Friendly Riads in Marrakech

Published on May 15, 2024 by Sarah Thompson

Discover the hidden gems of Marrakech where luxury meets sustainability. These carefully selected riads combine traditional Moroccan architecture with modern eco-friendly practices, offering travelers an authentic and environmentally conscious stay in the Red City.

1. Riad Yasmine

Located in the heart of the medina, Riad Yasmine has become an Instagram sensation for its stunning green courtyard, but its beauty is more than skin deep. The riad implements:

  • Solar water heating systems
  • Organic garden supplying the kitchen
  • Water recycling system for the courtyard plants
  • Local artisan-made furniture and decorations

2. Riad BE Marrakech

This beautifully restored riad combines traditional architecture with modern sustainable practices:

  • Energy-efficient LED lighting throughout
  • Rainwater harvesting system
  • Locally sourced organic toiletries
  • Zero-waste kitchen policy

3. Riad Dar Zaman

A small but perfectly formed riad that proves luxury can be sustainable:

  • Traditional passive cooling architecture
  • Local community employment initiatives
  • Plastic-free policy
  • Educational programs for guests about sustainable tourism

4. Riad Les Yeux Bleus

This stunning riad showcases how luxury and sustainability can coexist:

  • Natural swimming pool with chemical-free filtration
  • Solar-powered heating and cooling
  • Organic vegetable garden
  • Partnership with local environmental organizations

5. Riad Jardin Secret

A hidden paradise that takes sustainability seriously:

  • Extensive permaculture garden
  • Traditional water conservation methods
  • Recycling and composting programs
  • Support for local artisans and craftspeople

Booking Tips

  • Book directly with the riad when possible to support local businesses
  • Ask about their specific environmental initiatives
  • Consider visiting during shoulder season to reduce tourism impact
  • Request information about their community involvement
